I code, design, and think web.

My journey writing zeros and ones in this web universe began over 10 years ago writing Actionscript and HTML. Two years later I started coding backend and building Content Management Systems in my free time. My programming life started with the web, and thus I've grown accustom to being a web centric thinker. I'm an addicted learner by nature, and continually refine and evolve my knowledge of web development, making for relevant, coherent, creative and dynamic websites.

Over the years I've worked with a number of brands including Nike, FiestaBowl, Statefarm, Desert Botanical Garden, and Arizona Office of Tourism. I currently work with Infusionsoft. And, when I'm not programming you can typically find me with a book in my hand, learning something new, or pondering what it means to be human.
